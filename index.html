Success Snapshot

A one-page tool to help patients and clinicians define what success means beyond the number on the scale.

This is not a contract. It is a shared reflection tool. Weight, labs, and medical outcomes still matter, but so do energy, function, self-esteem, sleep, confidence, and daily life.
